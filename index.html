Welcome!

I'm Troy Baker, computer science professional with a masters in CS.

As a professional, I've worked at Google and Sandia National Laboratories. I've been a full stack developer and developed a passion for web design, database design, software architecture, and authentication & authorization. I contribute a strong vision and leadership to the projects in which I'm involved. My passion is designing solutions that deliver exceptional user experience and ease of use.

As a student, I developed a strong background in machine learning, theory of computation, and computer graphics. In graduate school, I researched rigidity theory (in the field of combinatorial geometry.) In addition to the purely theoretical aspect of this research, I also spent time developing novel algorithms and working on several programming projects of various scale. I also developed a passion for teaching, which I have found to be invaluable in every setting.

To me, computer science is more than a job. It is the best way to be a perpetual student and to express myself creatively. Every project provides the chance to learn things both inside and outside my domain. It gives me the freedom to invent and design solutions that work for myself and others. I take great pride in what I create and am grateful for every opportunity I have to pursue this passion.
